The Vichaara Huddle-Up

One day workshop that introduces your child to timeless self-enquiry principles. The workshop has 5 modules that are specifically designed to engage the active and absorbent minds of children between the ages of 6 and 12.

Module 1: The Call
Through a set of activities, introduces your child to the idea of 'Who Am I'.

Module 2: Existence
A guided activity to help your child articulate problems that (s)he faces today.

Module 3: Consciousness
A fun game that in the end helps your child discover a magic key, within.

Module 4: Aspiration
Five-finger activity to help your child find inner strength and follow their dreams.

Module 5: Silence of the Mountains
Role-based play, synthesizing what your child learnt into a memorable story.

To maximize individual attention and results, each huddle-up is limited to only 25 children. Seats will be reserved on a first-come-first-serve basis.

Gayathri

Creative Conscious Organizer

Hello, thanks for visiting and reading this website. I am Gayathri, and I am 52 years young. Until a few months ago, I spent over 25 years specializing in global sales and marketing for MNC’s.

I am your typical ‘girl next door’, nothing to shout about. Born into a middle class Tam-Brahm family in Madurai, I grew up with strong values and rich culture. I wedded my first love and moved to Chennai, had two great sons and now the most beautiful grand daughter who is sheer inspiration for this project!

Amidst the hustle-bustle of corporate and family life, I existed..merely existed, doing everything ‘compulsively’.

Then there was a ‘calling’, deeply stirring me to do something meaningful. I questioned my inner realm, lapped up works of Paramaatamaas like Ramana Bhagwan, Shir Aurobindo, Jiddu Krishnamurthi, Sadhguru Jaggi, drew concepts from each and an increased conviction gave rise to the project ‘Vichaara’, a self-enquiry program, exclusively for children between the ages of 6 and 12, that would be fun and activity based, nevertheless profound in reaching across the message to explore the vast realm within them and naturally find the ‘magic key’ that is already within each of them.

I quit my job in September 2019, to pursue this vision, moved to my eco-friendly farm and put on my thinking cap!

So you see, self-realisation is nothing to be gained afresh, it is already there! We all just need that gentle push to discover it, and it definitely does NOT need a PhD in Child Psychology to impart this message. A “Realized Self”, carrying Love and Conviction, Time and Energy would do and I have these abundantly.

I am now doing things ‘consciously’. From Existence I am metamorphosing to Awareness!
